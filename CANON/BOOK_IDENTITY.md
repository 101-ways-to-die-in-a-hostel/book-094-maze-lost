# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 094
**Title:** No Exit
**Slug:** maze-lost
**Category:** Circumstantial/Random

## The Death (One Sentence)
An overconfident solo traveler gets hopelessly lost in the labyrinthine converted medina hostel and dies of dehydration in a sealed-off wing that nobody knew existed.

## Tagline
"He couldn't find the bathroom. They couldn't find his body."

## Themes
1. The arrogance of assuming foreign spaces work like home
2. The danger of refusing to ask for help
3. Ancient architecture as a trap for modern travelers

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 35%
- Horror: 45%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Travelers who pride themselves on their sense of direction, who refuse to use maps, who believe they can "figure it out." Anyone who has ever been lost in a hostel at 3 AM looking for the bathroom.

## Comparison Titles
- "House of Leaves" meets "An Idiot Abroad"

## Content Warnings
- Claustrophobia
- Dehydration/starvation
- Slow death
- Isolation
